## ðŸŽ¨ **LAYOUT PRINCIPLES APPLIED**

### **1. Consistent Spacing System**
- **Header Section**: `mb-8` (32px margin bottom)
- **Card Grids**: `gap-6` (24px gap between items)
- **Form Fields**: `space-y-4` or `space-y-6` (16-24px vertical spacing)
- **Section Padding**: `p-6` (24px all around)
- **Content Padding**: `px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8` (responsive horizontal padding)

### **2. Grid Layouts**
- **Stat Cards**: `grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-4`
- **Data Cards**: `grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3`
- **Form Fields**: `grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2` (two-column on desktop)

### **3. Visual Hierarchy**
- **Page Heading**: 3xl font, gradient text, prominent positioning
- **Section Headings**: Text-lg or text-xl with proper spacing
- **Card Titles**: Text-lg with consistent styling
- **Body Text**: Text-sm with good contrast

### **4. Responsive Design**
- Mobile-first approach (stacks vertically on small screens)
- Tablet optimization (2-column layouts)
- Desktop enhancement (3-4 column layouts)
