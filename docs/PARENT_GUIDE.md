## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

Your school will provide you with login credentials:

1. **Receive Welcome SMS/Email** with:
   - Website link

**Fee Structure:**
Your child's fees are broken down into:
- **Tuition Fees:** Main school fees
- **Lunch Program:** If enrolled
- **Transport Fees:** If using school transport
- **Boarding/Hostel Fees:** If boarding


**Color Coding:**
- üü¢ Green: Fully paid
- üü° Yellow: Partially paid
- üî¥ Red: Overdue

### Viewing Fee History

1. Go to **Fees** tab in child's profile
2. View:
   - Current balance
   - Payment history (all payments made)
   - Fee structure details
   - Due dates
   - Penalties (if any)

### Payment Methods

The system supports multiple payment options:

#### 1. MPesa Payment (Recommended) üì±

**Step-by-Step MPesa Payment:**

1. **Go to Fees Tab**
   - Click "Pay Fees" button
   - Select "MPesa" as payment method

2. **Enter Payment Details:**
   - Amount to pay (minimum KES 100)
   - Your MPesa phone number (format: 0712345678 or 254712345678)
   - Select fees to pay (if multiple items)

3. **Initiate Payment:**
   - Click "Pay with MPesa"
   - Wait for STK Push notification on your phone

4. **Complete on Phone:**
   - Check your phone for MPesa prompt
   - Enter your MPesa PIN
   - Confirm payment

5. **Confirmation:**
   - System shows "Processing..." (wait 10-30 seconds)
   - You'll receive MPesa confirmation SMS
   - School system updates automatically
   - Receipt generates automatically

6. **Get Receipt:**
   - Receipt appears on screen
   - Download PDF receipt
   - Receipt also emailed to you

**Important MPesa Notes:**
- Ensure you have sufficient MPesa balance
- Use registered MPesa line
- Don't close browser while processing
- If payment fails, try again after 2 minutes
- Keep MPesa confirmation code for reference

#### 2. Cash Payment üíµ

1. Visit school accounts office
2. Make cash payment
3. Get official receipt
4. Payment is recorded in the system
5. You can view it in your portal within 24 hours

#### 3. Bank Deposit üè¶

1. Deposit to school bank account
2. Keep deposit slip
3. Visit accounts office with slip
4. Office will record payment
5. View in portal within 24 hours

#### 4. Cheque Payment üìù

1. Write cheque to school name
2. Submit to accounts office
3. Payment reflects after cheque clears (3-5 days)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Access

**Q1: I forgot my password. What do I do?**

**A:** 
1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Create new password
6. If you don't receive email, contact school office

**Q2: Can I have multiple people access my child's information?**

**A:** Yes, both parents/guardians can have separate accounts. Contact school admin to set up additional parent account.

**Q3: I have children in different schools. Can I use one account?**

**A:** No, each school has its own system. You'll need separate accounts for each school.

### Fees & Payments

**Q4: Why is my payment not showing after paying via MPesa?**

**A:** 
- Wait 1-2 minutes for system to update
- Refresh the page
- If still not showing after 10 minutes, contact accounts office
- Have your MPesa confirmation code ready

**Q5: Can I pay fees for the whole year at once?**

**A:** Yes, you can pay any amount up to the full balance. Overpayments carry forward to next term.

**Q6: I paid more than required. What happens?**

**A:** Overpayment automatically carries forward as credit to next term or next fee structure.

**Q7: Can I reverse an MPesa payment?**

**A:** Contact school accounts office immediately. MPesa reversals must be done by the school through Safaricom.

**Q8: Why am I being charged a late fee?**

**A:** Late fees apply when payment is not made by due date. Contact accounts office if you believe it's an error.

**Q9: Can I pay with international money transfer?**

**A:** Yes, contact accounts office for bank details. Payments reflect after transfer completes (3-7 days).

### Academic Information

**Q10: When will report cards be available?**

**A:** Report cards are published 1-2 weeks after end of term exams. You'll receive SMS notification when available.

**Q11: Can I see my child's marks before report cards?**

**A:** This depends on school policy. Some schools allow real-time mark viewing; others only with report cards.

**Q12: I disagree with my child's marks. What do I do?**

**A:** Contact the subject teacher through the messaging system. Request mark verification. Teacher or admin can review.

**Q13: Can I see my child's homework assignments?**

**A:** Yes, if teachers post assignments online, they appear in your child's profile under "Assignments" tab.

### Attendance

**Q14: My child was present, but marked absent. What do I do?**

**A:** Contact class teacher immediately through messaging system. Provide date and details. Teacher can correct attendance with admin approval.

**Q15: How do I inform school if my child will be absent?**

**A:** 
1. Send message to class teacher in advance
2. Or call school office
3. Provide reason for absence
4. For medical absence, submit doctor's note

**Q16: Why didn't I receive SMS when my child was absent?**

**A:** 
- Check notification settings
- Verify phone number is correct
- Contact school to enable absence SMS

### Communication

**Q17: How long do teachers take to respond to messages?**

**A:** Teachers typically respond within 24-48 hours on school days. For urgent matters, call school directly.

**Q18: Can I get the teacher's personal phone number?**

**A:** For privacy and professionalism, use the system messaging. School office can provide emergency contacts if absolutely necessary.

**Q19: I'm not receiving SMS notifications. Why?**

**A:** 
- Verify phone number in your profile
- Check notification settings
- Ensure number has credit/active
- Contact school IT support

### Technical Issues

**Q20: The website is not loading. What do I do?**

**A:** 
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing (Ctrl + F5)
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Contact school IT support

**Q21: I can't download receipts/report cards. What's wrong?**

**A:** 
- Enable pop-ups for the website
- Check if PDF viewer is installed
- Try different browser
- Check download folder (may have auto-downloaded)
- Contact IT support

**Q22: Can I use the system on a tablet?**

**A:** Yes, the system works on tablets, smartphones, and computers.

**Q23: Is my information secure?**

**A:** Yes, the system uses bank-level security with encrypted connections, secure passwords, and data protection measures.
